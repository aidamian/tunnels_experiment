## Direct-Client Conclusion
- The current public published-TCP hostnames cannot be used as native PostgreSQL or Bolt sockets without a local bridge/helper.
- Supported no-custom-bridge option: Cloudflare WARP plus private-network routing and a private hostname or IP.
- Public-FQDN native TCP would require a different product/topology; Cloudflare's current Spectrum-to-Tunnel integration does not fit this Bolt/PostgreSQL-through-Tunnel design unchanged.
